Aaron Kirtland

I am a first year PhD student in applied math at Brown University researching reinforcement learning. I am also interested in learning about computational cognitive science and computational neuroscience.

I graduated in 2021 from Washington University in St. Louis, where I studied math, computer science, and Chinese. I then took a gap year, during which I solo thru-hiked the Pacific Crest Trail and studied in Taiwan. Feel free to reach out if you would like to chat!
